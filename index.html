About Me

Emily believes in fully embracing love, our differences & our abilities. She has 15 years of experience in various areas of the culinary industry & graduated as a member of Phi Theta Kappa National Honor Society from LCC Culinary Arts. She is the mother of 4 children which has taught her how to slow down to speed up when it comes to progress. She found her love of web design in 2017 after creating her first website for a custom cabinetry company. After seeing the growth of the company from having an informative website and social media strategy she was hooked. Now, Emily works primarily with women-owned businesses, crowdfunding projects, & anyone that is looking to do good in the world. She traded her chef coat for design software and hasn’t looked back. You can still find her in the community volunteering time where she is needed or working on the next book in her children’s book series, “Bubbs in Space”, about autism awareness & acceptance.
